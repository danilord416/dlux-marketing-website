The Problem & The Solution

Wasted ad spend, unclear results, and campaigns that don't scale are common when paid media isn't managed with strategy and measurement. Without proper conversion tracking and A/B testing, you're guessing—not optimizing.

DLux runs data-driven digital advertising across Search, Display, and Video. We set up conversion tracking, run structured A/B tests for ROAS optimization, and continuously refine targeting and creative so your budget drives real business outcomes.

What's Included

Search campaigns — Capture high-intent users on Google (and Bing) with keyword strategy, ad copy, and landing pages aligned to conversions.

Display & banner advertising — Reach and retarget audiences across the web with visually consistent, on-brand creative.

Video advertising — YouTube and in-stream video campaigns that build awareness and drive action with clear CTAs.

Conversion tracking setup — Proper tagging and attribution so you know which channels and campaigns drive leads and sales.

A/B testing for ROAS optimization — Systematic tests on audiences, creatives, and landing pages to improve return on ad spend.

Campaign strategy & optimization — Ongoing bid management, audience refinement, and performance analytics and reporting.

Multi-platform management — Google Ads, Facebook & Instagram, LinkedIn, and other paid channels, coordinated for maximum impact.
