The Problem & The Solution

Keeping up with multiple platforms, content calendars, and engagement is time-consuming. Inconsistent or off-brand posts can dilute your message and miss opportunities to connect with customers.

DLux manages your social presence with a clear strategy: content planning, platform management, community engagement, and reporting. We create and curate content that fits your brand and audience so you stay visible and build real relationships—without the daily grind.

What's Included

Content strategy & planning — A content calendar and themes that align with your goals and audience.

Platform management — Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok, and others, tailored to where your audience is.

Community engagement & response — Timely replies, DMs, and comments so your community feels heard.

Content creation & curation — Original posts, stories, reels, and curated content that fits your voice and goals.

Social media analytics & reporting — Clear metrics and insights so you know what's working and what to adjust.

Influencer partnerships — Identification and coordination of partnerships that extend your reach and credibility.

Brand voice development — A consistent tone and messaging across all channels.

Social media advertising — Paid social campaigns that complement organic efforts and drive results.
